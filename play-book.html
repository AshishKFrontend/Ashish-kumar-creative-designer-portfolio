My Design Playbook

Design Philosophy

I believe that great design is that which is **unconventional** yet **intuitive** and **functional** for the user. I prioritize Simplicity and Accessibility.

The Process

Every successful project follows a systematic process. Here is my 5-step process:

  • **Discovery & Research:** Understanding the problem and identifying user needs.
  • **Strategy & Wireframing:** Defining the structure and user flow.
  • **Visual Design & Prototyping:** Creating high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes.
  • **Handoff & Collaboration:** Providing clear guidelines and assets to developers.
  • **Testing & Iteration:** Taking feedback from real users and refining the design.

Tools & Expertise

I use the following tools to bring projects to life:

  • **UI/UX Design:** Figma (Primary Tool), Adobe Creative Suite.
  • **Animation & Motion:** GSAP, After Effects.
  • **Front-End:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, SCSS/Sass.
